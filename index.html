How It Works

Step 1: Sign Up

First, you need to sign up either as a renter or a lender. Provide your details and verify your documents. Choose a membership plan if you wish to lend vehicles.

Step 2: Renting a Vehicle

If you're a renter, browse through the available vehicles in your area. Select the vehicle you want, book it, and proceed with payment. You can also opt for emergency assistance if needed.

Step 3: Lending a Vehicle

If you're a vehicle owner, you can lend your vehicle by listing it on our platform. Once it's approved, others can rent it. Earn money from the rentals, and track your earnings.

Step 4: Emergency Assistance

If you're renting a vehicle, you can request emergency help in case of an issue like a breakdown or medical emergency. Simply fill in the form, and our support team will assist you.
